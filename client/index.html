Field Guide

Progression

Punch logs, craft planks and sticks, make a table, then work toward stone tools, furnace, iron, and diamonds.

Threats

Zombies roam beyond the walls at night. Craft swords, place torches, and sleep at the tavern to reach dawn.

Gates

Portals open in the wild. Enter, slay the boss, and claim loot to unlock higher ranks and key tiers.

Town

Villagers offer quests, market stalls trade in gold, and Greta sells potions. Chests store your loot.

Shards

Bosses drop shards. Attune them at the plaza pedestal for harder gates and richer rewards.

Events

Use chat commands like /event king in beta testing, then join the queue from the server event banner.
